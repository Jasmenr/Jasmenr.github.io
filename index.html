About Me:

Jasmen Rocha is an aspiring Broadcaster who attends The University of Miami. Jasmen was born and raised in Miami, Florida. She has interned at Power 96, has been a guest host on Y100 with Michael Yo and has been an extra on Burn Notice and several Spanish Soap Operas/Series including Grachi on Nickelodeon. On her free time she likes to workout and spend time with her family.
